Philosophy


After many cycling events like Uci Grand Fondo and International tour of Rhodes and being part of the cycling world, Rhodes is ready for a new challenge , RC 258 Zeotec-RodosCycling.

Rhodes Island is the brilliant of Mediterranean sea, blessed by more than 300 shinny days every year, where the locals hospitality is routine and the famous Greek gastronomy approaches the maximum of traditional tastes. It has the amazing landscape that can be friendly for beginners but at the same time advanced cyclists can find great challenges as well. The wonderful climate & very good quality road network promise to any cyclist unforgettable holidays all year around.
RodosCycling company equipped by many years experiences on cycling events organizations feel ready to make the difference , and invite all the cycling lovers around the world to join this celebration “RC 258” on 4th May 2019.
Welcome to Rhodes island Your New Cycling Destination and enjoy cycling holidays.
