REGISTRATION RULES

In all distances the participation is open for all cyclists over 18 years old or over 16 with parent consent.

1.

RC 258 Zeotec RodosCycling , RC 162 & RC 80 are cycling events under Greek Cycling Federation , UCI and Cycle Tourism for everyone regulations as a free speed cyclosportive event where the individual participation and the effort are a motivation for self improvement. There will be classification list for all the participators who will complete the race and a special diploma of RC 258, RC 160 & RC 80 will be given to them with the distance and the approximate time reminding that the main reason of their effort except the physical exercise is the leisure and the culture of tourism.

2.

A prior registration for any kind of race is mandatory . The organization will have bib number plate for every participator. The participation fee for everyone is 50 euro. There is an insurance cost 10 euro and will be offered for free by RodosCycling company. The members of Greek Cycling Federation is mandatory to show their license.

3.

In order someone to participate in the race mast be member of Greek Cycling Federation or to acquire one day insurance that offered from the organization. Everyone cyclist participate by his own risk and responsibility of the participants. During the registration the participants declare that are healthy and have the physically fit to take part in the event.

4.

Registration for the event and payment can be done only by internet. Only paid subscriptions will be accepted.

5.

The registration is strictly personal, not transferable and none else can use it except the person registered. Only the participant can collect his number appearing a valid identification ( ID or Passport ) and none else.

6.

Every participant will have a number and mast be placed in the front of his bike . The plate of the number will have a chip attached so the timing system will give him classification and the ability to receive his participation diploma . Wrong position of the number angle may be will cause problem to timing system.

7.

The car in the end of the race will pick up the numbers and the timing chip from the riders who are out of the finish time limit . After this the cyclist can be transferred to the finish by sweeper car or by his own following the traffic rules without any assistance from the organization.

8.

Participation numbers and extra items will be received from the organization in the Race Hotel on 2nd May from 09.00 – 13.00 & 17.00 – 20.00 or on 3rd May from 09.00 till 21.00. After these time limits none can receive numbers or anything else . The numbers have to be in the front of the bike and mast be clearly visible if not police or organization staff can exclude these riders from the race. Numbers and the chips can be used only from the registered participant .

9.

If any participant seen towed on any vehicle will be excluded by the organization from the event. Service cars will be the race for technical support . Privet service cars from the participants or anyone else will not be allowed to follow the race from the police and they will be responsible for any fine resulting from not complying with these regulations. The organization reserve the rights to take action against anyone who brake the rules and will excluded from any future events.

10.

The exact locations of hydretion zones and feed zones will be anounced in the race book and in the site of the race.

11.

During the race event the organization will use team of doctors and abulances will move in all the lenght of the race, fixed aid stations and medical post in the arrival area.

12.

Service areas will be in certain locations , the support from the mechanics will be free of charge but for any materials will be used the participant will be charged.

13.

Equipment :

* Is obligated to wear a helmet.
* Is recomended but not obligated to wear the shirt of the race , that helps the organization staff to protect and support the participants.
* For security reasons the participants can use only road bikes that have brakes to both wheeels without time trial or triathlon handlbars. Fixie, triathlon, time trial or e-bikes will not allowed to participate.

14.

The Rhodes Traffic head office, Municipal Police and security guards will ensure the sfety of the event . The organization advised by Civil Protection Organization , may order partial or total suspention of the event if it has sufficient information on risks that may affect the safety of participants or collaborating personnel . These risks may be assosiated with Adverse weather phenomena ( storms, heavy winds, hailings, etc) floods, landslides, terrorist threat, etc or any other incident outside the cycling tour that could potentially affect it. Any different directions will be anounced and will be clearly marked by arrows and pannels.

All the participants must follow the provisions of the road safety actand its regulations. Each participant declares to know the event‘s regulations . By means with this document , participants declarethat they assume the risks involved in the event and that the organizationshall not be liable for any incident or accident resulting from taking part in the event.

16.

The Organization shall not be liable for any accident that may be caused byor involved a participant , who must be covered by the federated insurance , nor is it liable for any expences or depts that may be incured during the event by participants, nor for any loss or damageregarding bicycles or other equipment of the participants. The organization will be exempt from any liability for loss or damageof personal property under any circumstances.

Without prior notice the Organization reserve the right to make any appropriate changes if deemed necessary for any reson. It also reserves the right to Refuse Admission. Merely registering for this event implies acceptance of these regulations and waiving of all rights regarding the Organization, renouncing any legal action that might result from participation in the event. By registering the participant confirms that he accepts and agrees to respect the regulations of UCI and the Greek National Cycling Federation, as well as the particular regulations of the event. It also undertakes to accept the guidelines of the organization staff and of the security and first aid services, the risks associated with their participation, especially health risks, fall or collision, as well as risks related to road traffic and adverse weather conditions, where appropriate. 20.

Participants will be strictly penalized in the following cases :

*  If not cycle through the departure checkpoint and the other checkpoints located along the route
*  Locate themselves at departure locations that are not permitted by their participant number
*  Do not respect the Highway code
*  Do not respect the security procedures given by the police and by the Organization
*  Have their own escort vehicle, hindering the progress of the rest.
*  Dirty or degrade the route by throwing away rubbish.
*  Behave in a way that stops the event from being carried out as normal and that contravenes these regulations.
*  Members of the Police and of the Organization will take note of those who behave as mentioned above.
*  The Organization has a commitment with other participants and the authorities to penalize such participants effectively.

The penalty will be : 

•  Disqualification from the event without the right to receive a diploma or medal.
•  Inclusion on the list of penalized participants, for later publication.
•  A ban of taking part in future events.
•  Any penalties imposed by the authorities , warranted as a resault of the attitude taken.

21.

The Organization will not be liable if, without prior notice the event is suspended , the courses are modified or it is postponed due to force majeure.

22.

Winners must pick up the trophies at the podium as they will not be sent by post.
