VOLUNTEERS

You cannot participate at the RC258 but you would like to experience its atmosphere from within? Become a volunteer!
Volunteers are, together with our participants, the real protagonists of the RC258. They look after the cyclists at each feed point and they encourage them to move on.
Throughout the day, they carry the supplies, fill the cans, distribute the energy bars and the sunscreen, but they mainly remain in the memory of the athletes because they are the biggest fans when the miles are no longer so funny.

RC258 volunteers receive:
•  1 t-shirt of the RC258
•  1 cap of the RC258
•  food and drinks the day of the event
