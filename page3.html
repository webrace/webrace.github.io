General

 What is  RC258 and when will take place ?

RC 258 Zeotec-RodosCycling is a long distance cyclosportive event with almost 4000 meters elevation gain that gives the opportunity to all participants to discover Rhodes island fully of beauty and 2500 years history.

Someone can choose RC162 or RC80 as alternative distance choice and will take place on 4th May 2019 in Rhodes.

 From where is the start and the finish of RC258, RC162 & RC80 ?

The start and the finish for RC258,RC162 & RC80 is from Diagoras stadium Velodrome.

 Time and feed zones ?

The RC258, RC162 & RC80 start at 07.00.
The RC258 has 5 feed zones , RC162 has 4 feed zones and RC80 has 1 feed zone.

 What is the time limit of every race ?

The limit time of RC 258 is 20.00, of RC162 is 16.00 and of RC80 is 12.00.

 I am not sure now if I can complete the RC258 on May, when I can decide the route?

According the regulations you can decide even the last moment what distance you feel able to complete, so keep on training without stress.

Participation

 Who can participate in RC258 ? What is the minimum and what maximum of age?

Everyone in good health can participate if he likes to be joined in cycle challenges. The minimum age is 18 years old but is possible from 16 years old if there is written parental consent . There is not maximum age for participation 

 If I haven’t cycle license , do I have insurance in case of accident ?

If you haven’t license you can participate in the event being insured by one day insurance from the organization sponsor RodosCycling.

 Do I need medical certificate in order to participate?

The current legislation for cycling contests allows the participation without medical examination. Nonetheless, all participants are advised to do a medical examination before participating in the RC258 since it requires a great physical effort for a long time. When registering, participants declare that they are physically fit to take part in the event.

Registration

 How can I register to RC258 and what includes?

There will be available registration and accommodation packages from 1st January 2019, registration opening from 10th January 2019. The registration of RC258 includes a starting bag with a t-shirt and sponsor’s presents, The finishers will receive medals and a digital certificate with their race result.

 Are there any special accommodation offers for the RC258 ?

Yes there are . RodosCycling has to offer you the best offers for accommodation. For more info click here.

 If my schedule change and I am not able to participate in RC258 can I transfer my registration to another person?

The registration fee will NOT be refunded or transferred to another person. The registration is personal and non-transferable. Registered participants may only participate with the number that has been assigned to them and they agree not to allow others to use their number.
