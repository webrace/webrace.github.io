Diagoras cycling track stadium was built in 1932 from the Italian authorities and used like football stadium and cycling track. The first name of the stadium was Arena del Sole and later took the name of the Greek ancient Olympic winner Diagoras . The capacity of Diagoras stadium is 3.693 persons and many times during the Greek track championships had over 5.000 cycling funs.
